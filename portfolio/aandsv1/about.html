A&S stands for Abdul and Sham, two teenagers willing to give up their teenage years for their future. Instead of partying, hanging out with friends, going to concerts we decided to work now and as a reward for our hard work we could enjoy all these things later on in our life. We both became best friends in middle school and since then we have experimented with a ton of ideas and have a large number of skills under our belt, but nothing was more influential than starting A&S and working with our first client. This first project or client of ours motivated us to serve the businesses in our community to help them expand their online presence and to give them the ability to better serve their new and or existing customers. Many small business owners are not informed about the benefits of having a website for their business but we’re here to fill that gap and help you get started.
